Tag :-
    Tags in HTML are a series of characters wrapped in 
    "<" ">" to help structure a webpage these tags are 
    wrapped around some stuff to make elements.

Element :-
    An Element in HTML is just some stuff wrapped around
    in tags, here stuff could be some text, other 
    elements or a combination of both as well.

For Example: 
    <p> asdfgd <br> kjsahbd </p>
